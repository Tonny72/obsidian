## Last Modified Today

```dataview
TABLE string(split(string(file.mtime), " - ")[0]) as "Last Modified"
WHERE !contains(file.path, "2024-07-29")
  AND !contains(file.path, "2024-07-29 Daily Note")
  AND file.mday = date("2024-07-29")
SORT file.mtime DESC
```
