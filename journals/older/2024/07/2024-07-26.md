- ## Last Modified Today
  ```dataview
  TABLE string(split(string(file.mtime), " - ")[0]) as "Last Modified"
  WHERE !contains(file.path, "2024-07-26")
  AND !contains(file.path, "2024-07-26 Daily Note")
  AND file.mday = date("2024-07-26")
  SORT file.mtime DESC
  ```
