- #Postgresql
	- Download [[Postgresql]] en unzip.
	- Voer commando uit in Bin directory. Dit initialiseert de database
	- ```
	   ./pg_ctl -D "C:\Users\LEMTON00\Downloads\postgresql-17.2-2-windows-x64-binaries\pgsql\data" initdb -l logfile
	  ```
	- Om de service te starten
	- ```
	  ./pg_ctl -D "C:\Users\LEMTON00\Downloads\postgresql-17.2-2-windows-x64-binaries\pgsql\data" -l logfile start
	  ```
