## Journal
- ## Created Today
  
  ```dataview
  TABLE string(split(string(file.ctime), " - ")[0]) as "Created", file.mtime as "Last Modified"
  WHERE contains(file.path, "[[2023-12-06]]")
  OR (file.cday = date("[[2023-12-06]]") AND !regexmatch("^\d{4}-\d{2}-\d{2}", file.name))
  SORT file.name ASC
  ```
- ## Last Modified Today
  
  ```dataview
  TABLE string(split(string(file.mtime), " - ")[0]) as "Last Modified"
  WHERE !contains(file.path, "[[2023-12-06]]")
  AND !contains(file.path, "[[2023-12-06]] Daily Note")
  AND file.mday = date("[[2023-12-06]]")
  SORT file.mtime DESC
  ```
- ## Other Tasks Completed Today
  
  ```dataview
  TASK WHERE completion = date("[[2023-12-06]]")
  AND due != date("[[2023-12-06]]")
  ```
